The Best Movies According to Rebecca

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Princess Bride

This movie is a timeless classic and was pivitol in the foundation of my humor growing up. Arguably the most quotable movie of all time.

Interstellar

One of the greatest space movies of all time. Christopher Nolan made it feel like you were actually on the adventure with the characters. I saw this in theaters three times. Also, this soundtrack slaps.

Spider-man: Into the Spiderverse

The way this movie integrated a classic comic book art style into the storytelling made me giddy. The colors, the action, all of it. Miles Morales is also my favorite Spider-Man.
